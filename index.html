About Travelstart

Who are we?

Travelstart is Africa’s leading online travel agency, with offices across Africa and the Middle East. We aim to save you time, money and stress with our simple and convenient online booking platform. You can search, compare and book your travel all in one place. We believe you should spend less time planning your trip and more time enjoying your travels! Whether you’re looking for a domestic, international or last-minute flight, we’ll help you secure the best deal with our fantastic flight specials.

What do we do?

No matter how you prefer to travel, we offer something for every traveller’s taste and budget. Travelstart allows you to search and compare flights across a multitude of airlines, providing you with the lowest airfares on domestic and international flights. You can also find and book your perfect hotel as well as vehicle hire from top car rental companies. If you’d like to customize your holiday, our team of travel experts are just a phone call away for tailor-made holiday packages to anywhere in the world! Start comparing flights now and let us get you the best deal on flights, accommodation and car hire.

Why book with us?

Since its inception in 1999, Travelstart has become a pioneer in online travel. With our knowledge, passion and expertise, you can expect the best service and peace of mind when booking with us. We’ve partnered with the best in the industry, allowing us to offer you fantastic travel and flight deals. Skip the queues and spending hours trying to compare quotes online. With our convenient search tools, you can compare airlines and book your tickets at the click of a button. Our multiple payment methods allow you flexibility, should your travel plans change. In addition to providing great travel deals, cheap flights, accommodation and car rentals, we also offer travel insurance and VISA services.

With our exceptional customer service and expertise, it’s easy to see why millions of people choose to book with Travelstart!
